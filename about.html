About

Hi, I'm Jayne.

I build automation and AI systems — the kind that get tested against real execution logs and real edge cases, not just demoed once and called finished. I care more about whether something actually holds up than whether it looks impressive in a first pass.

How I work

I test what I build

Real execution logs, real edge cases, real failure modes — before calling anything "done." A build isn't finished until I've tried to break it.

I disclose what's fragile

Every project I ship comes with an honest account of its current limitations — hosting costs, access gaps, things still worth hardening. Overselling finished work isn't the goal; being trusted is.

No one-size-fits-all

I spend time studying each client's actual business problem before building anything — the fix that works for one business rarely works identically for another. The solution gets built around the problem, not the other way around.

I don't chase the crowd

I'd rather build real depth in an early, less-saturated skill than compete in an oversaturated one. Judgment matters more to me than trend-chasing.
